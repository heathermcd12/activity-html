The Cutest Dog of All: The Labradoodle

All dogs are adorable in their own way, but Labradoodles seem to have this cuteness about them that makes you want to shriek out of pure happiness. For those that are allergic to dog hair, this dog is a godsend. Unfortunately, 10-15% of canadians are allergic to dog hair, which often means that they may never have the excitement of owning a dog. Luckily for them, there are a range of different dog breeds that are hypoallergenic.

Much like the Poodle, there are three main sizes for the Labradoodle: standard, medium and miniature. Due to its hybrid nature, however, the physical characteristics of a Labradoodle may vary. For instance, a Labradoodle's coat may be wiry, wooly, wavy, curly or fleece-like. The color of the coat also varies, including cream, gold, red, black, chocolate, brindle and multi-patterned. Contrary to belief, some Labradoodles do shed, though far less, and with less odor than that of a Labrador Retriever.

Other reasons to get a Labradoodle

  1. They're intelligent
  2. Extremely friendly
  3. Great with children
